ABOUT DOUGLAS DC-3

The Douglas DC-3 is a propeller-driven airlainer manucfactured by the Douglas Aircraft Company, which had a lasting effect on the airline industry in the 1930s to 1940s and World War II.
The DC-3 had many exceptional qualities compared to previous aircraft. It was fast, had a good range, was more reliable and carried passengers in greater comfort.
